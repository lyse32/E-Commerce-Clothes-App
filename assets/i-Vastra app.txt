Ivastra shopping app is startup built by a team of Nano particles researcher to bring to you Smart-Clothes or I-Vastra.
Smart-Clothes are a combination of technology and trending fashions that meets for you.
Basically these clothes are coated with nano silver particles and various kinds of ethers
chemically produced at labs to provide you self cleanable clothes.
These chemicals are chemically inactive and hydrophobic and anti-bacterial in nature as a result these help in preventing 
any stain or dirt even smelling caused due to over sweating at work throughout the day,it also provides relief from washing 
buckets of clothes everyday as these can be cleaned by just wipping off wet clothes over its surface.
